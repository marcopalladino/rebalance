Welcome

Rebalance Experience is guided by Rebecca, who holds a Master's degree in Advanced Sports Science and Techniques, with experience in functional training and rehabilitation.

Each session on the beach or in the parks of Gran Canaria is designed to offer a gentle, immersive and professional experience, suitable for everyone: young people, adults, seniors and tourists.

"I created Rebalance Experience to share the joy of mindful movement, helping anyone rediscover balance and wellbeing in everyday life."

What is Rebalance Experience

"Balance: the poetry of the body listening to itself."

Rebalance Experience is not just physical exercise: it's a true journey to rediscover the beauty of every movement, the rhythm of breathing and presence in every moment.

On the beach or in the parks of southern Gran Canaria, every gesture becomes an opportunity to find harmony, strength and lightness, guided with professionalism and gentleness.

Here, movement becomes experience, awareness and wellbeing.

Why this name

The name "Rebalance" embodies the idea of finding your natural balance again: body and mind in harmony, strength and lightness integrated in every movement.

Rebalance Experience reminds us that balance is not a static state, but a continuous journey: every gesture, every breath, every step brings us back to our centre, where energy and serenity meet.
